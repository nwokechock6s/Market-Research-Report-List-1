<p><p>The Thermal Inkjet Encoder market primarily involves two key types of laser technologies: Fiber Laser and CO2 Laser. Fiber Lasers use optical fibers to generate high-intensity beams, ideal for marking and coding on various materials, offering precision and efficiency. In contrast, CO2 Lasers utilize a gas mixture for laser generation, making them suitable for non-metallic materials such as plastics and wood. Both types offer distinct advantages in terms of speed, quality, and application versatility within the coding and marking industry.</p></p>
