<p><p>The Partial Knee Arthroplasty market is divided into two main types: Fixed Bearing and Mobile Bearing. Fixed Bearing implants have a stationary component that provides stability and support, often preferred for patients with a particular range of motion. In contrast, Mobile Bearing implants feature a moving polyethene liner that allows for greater natural knee movement and may reduce wear over time. Both types aim to preserve healthy tissue and provide effective treatment for localized knee arthritis while enhancing patient mobility and comfort.</p></p>
