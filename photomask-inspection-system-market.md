<p><strong>The Photomask Inspection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Photomask Inspection System market is categorized into Die to Die (DD) and Die to Database (DB) methods. The DD method involves comparing features on a photomask directly against the corresponding features on another die, ensuring high precision in defect detection. In contrast, the DB method uses a reference database containing known good patterns to identify deviations in the photomask. Both methods are crucial for maintaining the quality and reliability of photomasks in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
