<p><p>The heart attack diagnostics market includes various types of tests that help identify heart conditions. Electrocardiograms (ECGs) measure the electrical activity of the heart, detecting irregularities. Blood tests assess biomarkers released during a heart attack, indicating heart muscle damage. Echocardiograms use ultrasound waves to produce images of the heart, revealing structural issues and blood flow problems. Together, these diagnostic tools are crucial for timely and accurate detection of heart attacks, facilitating effective treatment and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
