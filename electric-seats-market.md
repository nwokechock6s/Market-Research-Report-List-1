<p><ul><li>Manual Adjustment</li><li>Power Adjustment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric seats market is divided into two main types: manual adjustment and power adjustment. Manual adjustment seats require occupants to physically manipulate levers or knobs to achieve the desired position, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, power adjustment seats utilize electric mechanisms, allowing users to effortlessly customize their seating position with the push of a button. This type often includes features such as memory settings, lumbar support, and heating capabilities, enhancing overall comfort and convenience in vehicles.</p></p>
