<p><strong>The Plaster Trimmer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Plaster Trimmer</li><li>Dry Plaster Trimmer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plaster trimmer market is segmented into two main types: wet plaster trimmers and dry plaster trimmers. Wet plaster trimmers are designed for use with freshly applied plaster, utilizing moisture to achieve smooth finishes and enhance adhesion. In contrast, dry plaster trimmers are used on dried plaster surfaces, focusing on precision and detail in shaping and leveling. Each type caters to different stages of plastering work, catering to the needs of professionals seeking efficiency and quality in their plaster finishing processes.</p></p>
