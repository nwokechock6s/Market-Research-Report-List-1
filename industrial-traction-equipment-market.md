<p><p>The Industrial Traction Equipment Market encompasses essential components used in electric traction systems. Traction transformers step down high voltage from the power grid for safe use in trains and trams. Traction motors convert electrical energy into mechanical energy, driving the vehicles. Traction converters facilitate the conversion of alternating current to direct current and vice versa, ensuring efficient energy utilization. Together, these components enable reliable and efficient operation of electric transportation systems, supporting the growing demand for sustainable transit solutions.</p></p>
