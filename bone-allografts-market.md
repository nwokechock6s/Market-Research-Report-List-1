<p><p>The bone allografts market consists of various types, including synthetic grafts, allografts, xenografts, and others. Synthetic grafts are man-made materials designed to mimic bone properties, enhancing healing. Allografts involve the transplantation of bone from one person to another, ensuring biocompatibility and reducing rejection risk. Xenografts are derived from animal bone, typically sourced from cows or pigs, providing an alternative when human tissue is unavailable. Other options may include combinations or innovative materials, catering to specific surgical needs and enhancing recovery outcomes.</p></p>
