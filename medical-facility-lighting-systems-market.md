<p><p>The medical facility lighting systems market comprises two primary types: LED surgical lamps and halogen surgical lamps. LED surgical lamps offer enhanced energy efficiency, longevity, and superior brightness, providing optimal visibility during surgeries. They produce less heat, ensuring patient comfort. In contrast, halogen surgical lamps deliver bright, white light, but typically have a shorter lifespan and generate more heat. Both types are essential for ensuring precise illumination during medical procedures, enhancing the effectiveness and safety of surgeries.</p></p>
