<p><p>The dermal fillers market is categorized into several types based on their composition. Hyaluronic Acid (HA) fillers are popular for their hydration and elasticity properties. Calcium Hydroxyapatite (CaHA) fillers provide a structural foundation and stimulate collagen production. Poly-L-lactic Acid (PLLA) fillers encourage collagen synthesis over time, creating a gradual effect. Poly-Methyl Methacrylate (PMMA) fillers are semi-permanent, offering longer-lasting results. Other fillers include various synthetic and natural substances, catering to diverse aesthetic needs and preferences in the cosmetic industry.</p></p>
