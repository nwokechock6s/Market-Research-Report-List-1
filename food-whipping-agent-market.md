<p><p>The food whipping agent market is segmented into two main types: powdered and liquid whipping agents. Powdered whipping agents are dry formulations that incorporate air efficiently when beaten, producing stable foams for whipped cream and desserts. They are often preferred for their longer shelf life and ease of storage. Liquid whipping agents, on the other hand, are ready-to-use solutions that provide convenience and consistent results, often used in bakeries and catering for quick preparation of whipped toppings and fillings.</p></p>
