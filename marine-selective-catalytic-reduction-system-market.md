<p><p>The Marine Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) System is a technology designed to reduce nitrogen oxide (NOx) emissions from marine engines, ensuring compliance with International Maritime Organization (IMO) regulations. By injecting a urea-based solution, SCR systems convert harmful NOx into harmless nitrogen and water, making them vital for the shipping industry as it strives to minimize its environmental impact.</p><p>The Marine Selective Catalytic Reduction System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.2% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by increasing regulatory pressures for cleaner shipping, the need for retrofitting older vessels, and the expansion of global trade, which heightens the demand for efficient and eco-friendly maritime solutions. </p><p>Latest trends in the market include advancements in SCR technology, such as improved catalyst formulations that enhance efficiency and reduce operating costs. Moreover, retrofitting existing vessels with SCR systems is becoming more commonplace, driven by the industry's shift towards green technologies.
