<p><ul><li>Round</li><li>Cruciform</li><li>Annular and pull down apex</li><li>Rogallo wing</li><li>Ribbon and Ring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Parachute market types refer to different designs used for aerial delivery systems. Round parachutes, with a simple shape, are widely used for heavy loads. Cruciform designs improve stability, while annular parachutes offer better drag efficiency. Pull-down apex parachutes enhance control during descent. Rogallo wings combine glider and parachute features for versatility. Ribbon parachutes provide slow descent rates, ideal for delicate equipment, while ring parachutes offer compact storage and stable landings. Each design serves specific operational requirements in aerial delivery.</p></p>
