<p><p>The dust mop market is primarily divided into two types: ordinary dust mops and electrostatic dust mops. Ordinary dust mops typically consist of cotton or microfiber fibers that require manual dusting, effectively capturing dust and debris on surfaces. In contrast, electrostatic dust mops use special materials that attract and trap dust particles through static electricity, making them more efficient in cleaning fine dust and allergens. Both types cater to different cleaning needs in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
