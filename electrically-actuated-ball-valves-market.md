<p><p>The electrically actuated ball valves market is categorized based on connection types, each catering to specific application needs. Threaded connections offer easy installation and maintenance for smaller pipes. Flanged connections provide robust sealing for larger pipes with higher pressure applications. Butt weld connections create a seamless flow by welding the valve directly to the pipe, ideal for permanent setups. Socket weld connections, similar to butt welds, allow for strong, secure attachments in high-pressure systems, ensuring reliability and performance.</p></p>
