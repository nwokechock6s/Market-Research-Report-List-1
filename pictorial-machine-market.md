<p><strong>The Pictorial Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Foaming Pictorial Machine</li><li>Piezo Pictorial Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pictorial machines are categorized primarily into thermal foaming and piezo machines. Thermal foaming pictorial machines utilize heat to create images on materials, often resulting in vibrant and durable prints ideal for textiles and packaging. In contrast, piezo pictorial machines deploy piezoelectric technology to eject ink droplets, offering precision and versatility for various substrates. Both types cater to distinct market needs, with thermal foaming emphasizing speed and image quality, while piezo machines provide flexibility and detail for intricate designs.</p></p>
