<p><p>The Flexible Printed Circuit Board (FPCB) market includes various types tailored for specific applications. Single-sided flex circuits comprise a single conductive layer, ideal for simple designs. Double-sided flex circuits have conductive material on both sides, allowing for more complex connections. Multi-layer flex circuits consist of multiple layers for high-density applications. Rigid flex circuits combine flexible and rigid substrates, enabling robust designs in compact spaces. Other types may include specialty circuits catering to unique industrial needs, enhancing versatility across sectors.</p></p>
