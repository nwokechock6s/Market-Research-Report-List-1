<p><p>The colon cancer molecular diagnostics market includes several key technologies that enable precise genetic analysis. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) amplifies specific DNA sequences to identify mutations. FISH (Fluorescence In Situ Hybridization) detects chromosomal abnormalities through fluorescently labeled probes. DNA sequencing provides detailed genetic information by reading nucleotide sequences, allowing for comprehensive mutation analysis. Gene chips, or microarrays, analyze the expression of multiple genes simultaneously, offering insights into tumor biology and aiding in personalized treatment strategies.</p></p>
