<p><p>The Milk and Dairy Analyzer market is primarily categorized into two types: ultrasonic and infrared. Ultrasonic analyzers utilize sound waves to assess the composition and quality of milk, providing accurate measurements of parameters like fat and protein content. In contrast, infrared analyzers employ infrared spectroscopy to identify and quantify various components in dairy products, enabling rapid analysis with minimal sample preparation. Both technologies play a crucial role in ensuring product quality and compliance within the dairy industry.</p></p>
