<p><p>The rhinoscope devices market comprises two main types: video rhinoscopes and fiber rhinoscopes. Video rhinoscopes utilize high-definition cameras to provide real-time imaging, enhancing visualization during diagnostics and procedures. They offer improved clarity and allow for documentation and analysis. In contrast, fiber rhinoscopes employ flexible fiber optics, enabling access to confined nasal passages with effective illumination. Both types are essential in otolaryngology for diagnosing and treating nasal conditions, with distinct advantages in terms of imaging quality and maneuverability.</p></p>
