<p><ul><li>Mammalian Protein Expression Systems</li><li>Baculovirus Protein Expression Systems</li><li>Prokaryotic Protein Expression Systems</li><li>Yeast Protein Expression Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Protein Expression Technology market encompasses various systems for producing proteins. Mammalian protein expression systems utilize mammalian cells for complex protein structures, ideal for therapeutic proteins. Baculovirus systems employ insect cells, offering high yield and post-translational modifications. Prokaryotic systems, such as bacteria, are efficient for rapid production but may lack modifications. Yeast protein expression systems balance eukaryotic characteristics with ease of fermentation, suitable for various applications. Each system offers unique advantages depending on the protein's complexity and intended use.</p></p>
