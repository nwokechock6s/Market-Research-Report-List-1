<p><p>Liquid chromatography instruments are analytical tools used to separate, identify, and quantify compounds in a mixture. Furthermore, increasing investments in research and development, particularly in the pharmaceutical and biotechnology sectors, are contributing to the rising adoption of liquid chromatography.</p><p>Latest trends indicate a growing focus on miniaturization and automation of instruments, enhancing efficiency and reducing analysis time. Ultra High Pressure Liquid Chromatography (UHPLC) offers faster analysis times and higher resolution by operating at higher pressures.
