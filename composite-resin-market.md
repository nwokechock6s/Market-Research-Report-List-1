<p><p>The composite resin market includes various types such as epoxy resin, unsaturated polyester resin, and phenolic resin, each serving distinct applications. Epoxy resin is known for its excellent adhesion, chemical resistance, and durability, making it ideal for aerospace and automotive industries. Unsaturated polyester resin is favored in construction and marine applications due to its versatility and cost-effectiveness. Phenolic resin offers high thermal stability and is often used in electrical and thermal insulation. Other resins encompass a wide range of specialized formulations for specific uses.</p></p>
