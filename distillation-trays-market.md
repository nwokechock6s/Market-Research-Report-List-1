<p><strong>The Distillation Trays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bubble Cap Trays</li><li>Sieve Trays</li><li>Valve Trays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The distillation trays market comprises primarily three types: bubble cap trays, sieve trays, and valve trays. </p><p>Bubble cap trays feature caps over bubble-shaped openings, enhancing vapor distribution and providing better contact between liquid and vapor. Sieve trays have perforated plates allowing vapor to rise freely through holes, offering simple design and lower pressure drop. Valve trays incorporate movable valves that adjust to vapor flow, improving efficiency and flexibility in operation. Each type serves specific applications based on efficiency, pressure, and separation requirements in distillation processes.</p></p>
