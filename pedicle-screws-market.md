<p><ul><li>Polyaxial</li><li>Monoaxial</li><li>Cannulated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pedicle screws market comprises three main types: polyaxial, monoaxial, and cannulated screws. Polyaxial screws feature a multidirectional head that allows for flexible angling during insertion, enhancing spinal alignment. Monoaxial screws have a fixed head designed for a single angle of insertion, offering simplicity and stability. Cannulated screws are hollow, facilitating guided placement over a wire, which enhances precision in surgery. Each type serves specific surgical needs and contributes to advancements in spinal fusion procedures.</p></p>
