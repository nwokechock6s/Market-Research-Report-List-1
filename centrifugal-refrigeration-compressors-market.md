Ordinary Centrifugal compressors are widely used due to their reliability and simplicity, using conventional mechanical bearings. Air Suspension Centrifugal compressors feature a unique design where air bearings reduce friction, allowing for smoother operation and minimal wear. Each type offers distinct advantages in efficiency, maintenance, and performance, catering to various industrial refrigeration needs.</p></p>
