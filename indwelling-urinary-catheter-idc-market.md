<p><p>The Indwelling Urinary Catheter (IDC) market encompasses various types, primarily including Indwelling Silicone Catheters and Indwelling Latex Catheters. The choice between these catheters depends on factors like duration of use, patient sensitivity, and the specific healthcare setting requirements.</p></p>
