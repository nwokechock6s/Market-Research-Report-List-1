<p><ul><li>Traditional Alkaline Electroliser</li><li>PEM Electroliser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water electrolysis equipment is categorized into two main types: traditional alkaline electrolyzers and PEM (Proton Exchange Membrane) electrolyzers. Traditional alkaline electrolyzers utilize an alkaline solution as the electrolyte, offering low cost and established technology, but with slower reaction rates and larger footprints. In contrast, PEM electrolyzers use a solid polymer membrane, enabling higher efficiency, faster response times, and compact designs, making them suitable for applications requiring rapid hydrogen production. Both technologies serve diverse needs in hydrogen generation and energy storage.</p></p>
