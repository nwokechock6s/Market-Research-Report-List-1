<p><p>High Performance Polyethylene (HPPE) is categorized into three market types based on density: Low Density (LDPE), Medium Density (MDPE), and High Density (HDPE). LDPE features flexibility and resistance to chemicals, commonly used in packaging. MDPE strikes a balance between strength and flexibility, suitable for gas pipes and packaging films. HDPE offers superior durability and rigidity, ideal for containers, toys, and industrial applications. Each type caters to specific operational needs, driving diverse applications across industries.</p></p>
