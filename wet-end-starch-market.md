<p><p>The wet-end starch market is characterized by various types, primarily cationic and amphoteric starches. Cationic starches are positively charged, enhancing paper strength, wet resistance, and retention during the papermaking process. They are widely used for improving drainage and dewatering. Amphoteric starches possess both positive and negative charges, offering versatility in applications such as paper coating and improving surface properties. Both types contribute significantly to the efficiency and quality of industrial processes in paper and packaging sectors.</p></p>
