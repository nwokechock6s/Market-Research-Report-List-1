<p><ul><li>Angle of Repose</li><li>Cylinder Test</li><li>Funnel Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automated Powder Flow Tester market comprises three primary test types: the Angle of Repose, Cylinder Test, and Funnel Test. The Angle of Repose assesses the flowability of powders by measuring the maximum angle at which they can remain stable. The Cylinder Test evaluates flow characteristics under controlled conditions, simulating industrial processing. The Funnel Test measures how quickly powder flows through a funnel, indicating its cohesiveness and potential blockages. Together, these tests provide critical insights for optimizing powder handling in various industries.</p></p>
