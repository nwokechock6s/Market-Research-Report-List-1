<p><p>The pneumatic impact wrench market is primarily segmented into two types: impact wrenches and pulse pneumatic wrenches. </p><p>Impact wrenches deliver high torque output through rapid rotational impacts, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications like automotive repair and construction. In contrast, pulse pneumatic wrenches utilize a unique mechanism that produces a series of short bursts of torque, allowing for more controlled tightening and reduced wear on fasteners. Both tools are essential in various industries for enhancing efficiency and reducing manual labor.</p></p>
