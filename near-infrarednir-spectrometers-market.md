<p><p>The Near Infrared (NIR) spectrometer market comprises several types, each serving distinct applications. Fourier Transform Near Infrared (FT-NIR) spectrometers offer high-resolution data for chemical analysis. UV-Vis-NIR spectrometers combine ultraviolet, visible, and near-infrared wavelengths for broader spectral analysis, ideal for quantitative measurements. Raman spectrometers leverage inelastic scattering of light for molecular identification, useful in pharmaceuticals and materials science. Additionally, "Others" may include handheld or portable designs, catering to diverse fields like agriculture and food safety, enhancing accessibility and usability.</p></p>
