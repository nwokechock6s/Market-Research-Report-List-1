<p><strong>The Fabric Protection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Seals in the Fibers</li><li>Penetrates the Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fabric protection market features two primary types: seals in the fibers and penetrates the fiber. Seals in the fibers involve applying a protective layer to the surface of textiles, creating a barrier against stains and spills without altering the fabric's appearance. Conversely, penetrates the fiber treatments are absorbed directly into the fabric, providing deep protection against stains and wear while maintaining breathability and texture. Both methods enhance fabric longevity, cleanliness, and aesthetic appeal, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
