<p><p>The inline mixer market is categorized into three main types: plastic mixers, carbon steel mixers, and stainless steel mixers. Plastic mixers are lightweight and corrosion-resistant, suitable for various applications, particularly in industries where chemical exposure is minimal. Carbon steel mixers are durable and cost-effective, ideal for handling abrasive materials but may require protective coatings to prevent rust. Stainless steel mixers offer excellent corrosion resistance and hygiene, making them preferred in food, pharmaceutical, and chemical industries where cleanliness is critical.</p></p>
