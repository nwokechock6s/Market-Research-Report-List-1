<p><p>The Rescue Helmets market is categorized into two main types: ABS Rescue Helmets and HDPE Rescue Helmets. ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) helmets are known for their durability and impact resistance, making them suitable for high-risk rescue operations. In contrast, HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) helmets offer excellent chemical resistance and lightweight properties, providing comfort during prolonged use. Each type serves specific operational needs, ensuring the safety and effectiveness of rescue personnel in various environments and situations.</p></p>
