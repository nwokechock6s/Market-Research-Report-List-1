<p><p>The semiconductor measuring equipment market is divided into two key categories: front-end and back-end equipment. Front-end equipment focuses on the initial stages of semiconductor manufacturing, including wafer fabrication, where precise measurements of electrical and physical properties are critical for ensuring device performance and yield. Back-end equipment deals with the final stages, such as packaging and testing, where the integrity and functionality of the finished semiconductor products are evaluated. Both types are essential for maintaining quality and efficiency in semiconductor production.</p></p>
